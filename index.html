Is DiploX.ai made specifically for Polytechnic students?

Yes! DiploX.ai is built exclusively for Polytechnic Diploma students. Our AI is trained on diploma-level curricula aligned with state technical boards like MSBTE, BTEUP, RSBTE, and HSBTE — not generic content.

Can I use DiploX.ai on my phone?

Absolutely. DiploX.ai is fully mobile-responsive and works seamlessly on any smartphone browser. A dedicated Android and iOS app is also in development and coming soon.

Is my data safe and private?

Yes. Your notes, uploads, and study data are fully encrypted and private. We never share or sell your personal information. You own all content you create on DiploX.ai.

Does DiploX.ai support Hindi medium students?

Hindi language support is actively in development and coming very soon. Once live, you'll be able to generate notes, summaries, and get answers in Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati, and other regional languages.

How accurate are the Prediction Papers?

Our AI analyzes previous year question patterns, chapter weightage, and board trends to generate high-probability question papers. Students report 60–75% question similarity on average. Results may vary by subject and board.

What file formats can I upload?

You can upload PDF, DOCX, and TXT files up to 25 MB. You can also simply paste text or type a chapter topic — no upload required.
